ENTER to confirm choice in menu

Left Shift + any combination below allows you to dash

W or Arrow Key Up to move up

A or Arrow Key Left to move left

S or Arrow Key Down ro move down

D or Arrow Key Right to move right

W+A/Arrow Key Up + Left to move up and leftwards at the same time

W+D/Arrow Key Up + Right to move up and righwards at the same time

S+A/Arrow Key Down + Left to move down and leftwards at the same time

S+D/Arrow Key Down + Right to move down and rightwards at the same time

Close window to quit

Game auto closes if you die or after 60 sec 

Enjoy
